Answers to the most common questions about working together.

The free 30-minute discovery call is a no-obligation conversation where we discuss your goals, challenges, and how I can help. I'll ask about your project or coaching needs, give you an honest assessment, and outline possible approaches. You'll leave with clarity on next steps — no pressure to proceed.

Yes, absolutely. All services are delivered remotely via video call, collaborative tools, and async communication. I work with clients across North America, Europe, Africa, and beyond. Time zone coordination is easy — I'm flexible with scheduling to accommodate different regions.

Project duration varies by scope. A data analysis report may take 1–2 weeks. A full-stack web application typically takes 4–12 weeks. Coaching engagements are usually structured in 4–8 week blocks with weekly sessions. I provide clear timelines during the strategy phase so you always know what to expect.

My coaching blends life literacy — practical skills like financial management, communication, and career strategy — with Bible-based principles for those who desire a faith-centered approach. Both tracks are grounded in real-world application, not just theory. I tailor every engagement to the individual, not a generic script.

For technical projects, I typically require a 50% deposit before work begins with the remainder due upon delivery. Coaching sessions are billed per session or in package bundles. All engagements include a simple service agreement outlining scope, deliverables, and payment terms. I accept credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal.
