Last updated: January 1, 2025

InsightSerenity ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by Emmanuel Oyemosu. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it. This policy applies to all visitors and clients of eolaw.github.io and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information collected automatically:

Information you provide:

Name, email address, and phone number when you fill out our contact form
Service preferences and project descriptions you provide
Scheduling information when you book through Calendly

Information collected automatically:

Browser type, operating system, and referring URL via server logs
Pages visited and time spent (if analytics tools are enabled)
Theme preference stored in your browser's localStorage (no server)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information solely to provide and improve our services:

To respond to inquiries and schedule consultations
To send confirmation emails and service-related communications
To deliver contracted consulting or coaching services
To improve the website and user experience
To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not use it for advertising or marketing beyond responding to your direct inquiry.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses minimal client-side storage:

localStorage (is-theme-override): Stores your light/dark mode preference locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.
Calendly cookies: The Calendly booking widget may set its own cookies to manage your scheduling session. Please see Calendly's privacy policy at calendly.com/legal/privacy-notice.
Formspree: Our contact form is processed by Formspree, which may set cookies to prevent spam. See Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or behavioral analytics tools on this website.

This site integrates the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

Calendly

Used for scheduling consultations. When you book through Calendly, their platform collects your name, email, and scheduling preferences. Policy: calendly.com/legal/privacy-notice

Formspree

Used to process contact form submissions. Your form data is transmitted securely to Formspree and then forwarded to our email. Policy: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
