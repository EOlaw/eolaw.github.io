Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how EOLAW Consulting collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with our services.

1 Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when booking consultations or contacting us. This includes:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone)
  • Company or organization details
  • Project descriptions and requirements
  • Communication preferences
  • Technical information about your project needs

We also collect non-personal data such as browser type, IP address, and pages visited through standard web analytics.

2 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to consultation requests and inquiries
  • Schedule and conduct consultation sessions
  • Prepare project proposals and technical assessments
  • Send relevant updates about your project (if engaged)
  • Improve our services and client experience

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing communications.

3 Information Sharing

EOLAW Consulting does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • With service providers essential to delivering our services (e.g., email providers)
  • With your explicit written consent

All third-party service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure, encrypted communication channels
  • Restricted access to personal data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Data minimization practices — we only collect what is necessary

Despite these measures, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to contact us only through secure channels.

5 Cookies & Tracking

This website uses minimal cookies for:

  • Session management and functionality
  • Anonymous analytics to improve user experience

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use our core services.

6 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any future communications
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at emmanuel.ao@outlook.com.

7 Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

Consultation records are typically retained for 2 years unless a longer engagement requires otherwise.

8 Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business clients aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, we will take immediate steps to delete that information.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email to active clients.

Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed.
